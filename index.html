José Rizal

Some Facts about José Rizal

  • His full name is José Protasio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda
  • He was a Filipino nationalist and polymath during the tail end of the Spanish colonial period of the Philippines.
  • Was called as the National Hero (Pambansang Bayani) of the Philippines.
  • Became a writer and a ket memeber of the Filipino Propaganda Movement, which advocated political reforms for the colony under Spain.
  • Was executed by the Spanish colonial government for the crime of rebellion after the Philippine Revolution
  • Widely considered one of the greatest heroes of the Philippines
  • Author of Noli Me Tangere and El Filibusterismo and a number of poems and essays

Timeline of José Rizal

  • 1861 | June 19th, born to Francisco Rizal Mercado y Alejandro and Teodora Alonso Realonda y Quintos in Calamba, Laguna. Had 9 sisters and 1 brother.
  • 1870 | Begins school under the instruction of Justiniano Aquin Cruz at 9 years old
  • 1871 | Continues education under Lucas Padua
  • 1872 | Examined College Entrace to St. Tomas University in Manila and enters as a scholar.
  • 1875 | Enters Ateneo at 14 years old. At the age of 15, He receives Bachelor of Arts Degree from Ateneo de Manila, he receives it with the highest honors possible.
  • 1882 | First travel to Europe
  • 1887 | Publication of Noli Me Tangere in Berlin.
  • 1891 | Publication of El Filibusterismo in Belguim.
  • 1892 | Exile in Dapitan.
  • 1892 | Founded La Liga Filipina in Philippines.
  • 1896 | Was arrested going to Cuba via Spain and was imprisoned in Barcelona. Was sent back to Manila on the same day. On December 30, 1896 He was executed, he was shot in Bagumbayan.
